We are moving from "Owner-Operator" (driving your own mower) to "Fleet Commander" (SimTower/Tycoon style).
In Version 3.0, we introduce:
The Drone Fleet: You can now purchase autonomous "Mini-Bots." These don't need fuel; they just roam and mow. They are slow and dumb, but they are loyal.
Contract System (Map Selection): You aren't stuck in the backyard anymore. You can now accept contracts for the City Park (lots of rocks) and the Golf Course (huge payouts, very fast growth).
Visual "Juice": Added particle effects. Grass explodes into little green pixels when cut. It makes the game feel much more alive.
Dynamic Camera: Since the maps are now larger than your screen, the camera smoothly follows your main mower (SimCity style).
