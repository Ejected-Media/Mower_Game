The Hybrid Design
Single Screen View (No Scrolling): Just like Version 1, the entire map fits on your screen. The tiles automatically resize: if you load a huge map (like the Golf Course), the tiles get smaller to fit everything in view. This restores that "Screensaver" feeling.
V1 Interface: The D-Pad is back on the main screen, always available.
V4 Features (Hidden Complexity): The deep features (Fleets, Hulls, Contracts) are tucked away in a "Shop" button so they don't clutter the arcade experience.
Action Focused: The "Sell" and "Refuel" buttons are big and right next to the D-Pad for quick tapping

Key features of the Arcade Edition:
Automatic Zoom: If you buy the "Golf Course" contract, the tiles will shrink to fit the whole course on your screen. If you play the "Backyard," they get big and chunky.
Hidden Depth: Tap the orange "SHOP / MAPS" button to find all the complex stuff (Contract buying, Drone purchases, Hull upgrades).
Visual Indicators: The "Sell" and "Refuel" buttons flash when you need to press them (Bag Full / Low Fuel).
