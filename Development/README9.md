4-Stage Grass Growth with Texture:
Grass now grows in 4 stages (0-3).
Stage 0 (Cut): Light green, no texture.
Stage 1-3 (Growing): Progressively darker shades of green with drawn grass blade lines for a textured look.
Cutting any growth stage immediately resets it to Stage 0.
"Cash" Currency:
The top HUD now labels your money as "Cash" instead of "Gold".
